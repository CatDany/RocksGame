How to Play

Your goal is to catch falling rocks.

Use Left Mouse Click and Right Mouse Click to move left and right respectively.

Gray rocks give you 1 point for catching, blue ones give you 4.

After the game is over, all your points are converted to credits. Credits are saved between sessions!
